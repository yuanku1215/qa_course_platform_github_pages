Course 101: Problem Definition and the 7 Axes

A compact map of optimization problems. We classify a problem by variables, constraints, geometry, time, uncertainty, interaction, and objectives. This helps you pick the right modeling choices and solvers later.

Lessons

    Practice

    7 short problems with solutions

    Each problem is designed to reinforce one axis. Try it first, then open hints and solutions.

    1 Variables: Mixed decision types Axis 1
    Question

    A system has an on or off switch y and a production level x. If the system is off, production is 0. If it is on, production must be between 10 and 50. Write constraints linking x and y.

    Hint

    Use y to activate the lower and upper bounds of x.

    Answer

    y ∈ {0,1},   10y ≤ x ≤ 50y

    2 Constraints: Exactly one choice Axis 2
    Question

    A commuter must choose exactly one mode: public transit or driving. Let x1, x2 be binary. Write the objective and constraint.

    Hint

    Exactly one means the sum of binary choices equals 1.

    Answer

    min 3x1 + 7x2

    s.t. x1 + x2 = 1, x1, x2 ∈ {0,1}
